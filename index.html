About


The EdTech platform provides a centralized hub where students can access meticulously curated course playlists from YouTube, ensuring they receive high-quality content tailored to their exam requirements. Upon selecting a particular subject, the platform displays the best available playlists sourced from YouTube, endorsed by educators or top performers in the respective field. Students have the flexibility to watch the entire playlist directly within the platform, facilitating seamless and uninterrupted learning. Additionally, to cater to the needs of time-constrained individuals, the platform incorporates an innovative feature: YouTube transcript summarization. This feature enables users to access summarized transcripts of the videos within the playlist, offering a concise overview of the key concepts covered in each video.
